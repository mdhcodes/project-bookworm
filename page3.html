Why do we need Project Bookworm?

In 2011, the Pew Research Center began gathering data on adult book-reading habits in the United States.

Since that time, the number of Americans who report reading at least one book in the past year has declined while the number of Americans who state that they didn't read a book has increased.

Project Bookworm wants to change that. We want to encourage non-readers to read and readers to read even more. Project Bookworm will help individuals find and locate books that will interest and engage them.

Since most Americans read books in print, we'll direct our users to stores and libraries where they can find their selections in print for free or for sale. We don't want a price tag to stop anyone from reading.

We want the Pew Research Center to report that all Americans read!
